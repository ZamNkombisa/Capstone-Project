Our Story

Fine Dine was born out of a vision to provide a simple, yet warm and comfortable dining experience. It all started with an idea and a passion to cook, a food lover, to bring people together for a pleasant dining experience, to create something I can share with others. With Chef Zamuxolo a natural artist, with an immense passion in culinary art, and Michael an accountant with a business passion, Fine Dine Restaurant was born.
